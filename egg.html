Fried Egg

(Good morning starter!)

Ingredients

  • Eggs x 3
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Cup of water (will be used later)

Steps

  1. Turn stove to medium heat and cover surface of olive oil
  2. crack eggs on pan and season with salt and pepper
  3. Place pan lid on top and pour water into pan
  4. Cover pan to allow steam to cook top of the eggs
  5. Wait until yolks are covered with a light wight filament and serve
