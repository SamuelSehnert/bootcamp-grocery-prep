Tofu Scramble

(Plantbased dish that tastes great!)

Ingredients

  • Firm Tofu x 1 packet
  • Spinach x 2 handfulls
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Tumeric x 1 tbs
  • Nutritional Yeast x 1/4 cup
  • Veggie bouillon x 1 tsp

Steps

  1. Crumble tofu in a bowl with a fork
  2. Add olive oil to medium heat pan
  3. While pan is heating up, mix all ingredients (not the tofu) into a bowl
  4. Add crumbled tofu to pan and pour sauce on top
  5. Mix until tofu is coated, add spinach and let sit, mixing occasionally
