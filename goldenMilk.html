Golden Milk

(Will knock the sickness RIGHT out of you!)

Ingredients

  • Oat/Almond milk x 1 cup
  • Tumeric x 2 tsp
  • Giger x 1 tsp OR 1 small grated chunk
  • Pepper x 1 pinch
  • (OPTIONAL) Honey/Agave x 2 tsp

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to bowl and mix until homogeneous
  2. Place on low heat and let simmer for 10 minutes
  3. Enjoy not being sick!!
