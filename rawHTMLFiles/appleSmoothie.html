Apple Pie Smoothie

(Because what's more American than that?)

1

Ingredients

  • 1 Apple
  • 0.5 tsp of Cinnamon
  • 0.5 tsp of Nutmeg
  • 1 scoop of Protein powder
  • Sweetener (Honey/Agave)
  • Oats to desired consistancy
  • Milk of some type (cow/nut/etc) to desired consistancy

Steps

  1. Cut apple into small chunks
  2. Add all ingredients to smoothie cup
  3. Add milk and blend for 30 seconds
  4. Check for desired consistancy, if too thin add more oats
